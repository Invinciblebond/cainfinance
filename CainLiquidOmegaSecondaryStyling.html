Strategy

The Cain Liquid II vault provides institutional investors with structured exposure to short-duration payment receivables originated by international payment and remittance businesses across Southeast Asia, MENA, and LATAM regions.


The strategy targets investment-grade credit quality with a working capital focus, providing consistent yield through a BVI-domiciled special purpose vehicle that finances cross-border payment infrastructure.
