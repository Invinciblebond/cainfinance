About

The aCain vault accepts USDC deposits on Solana and allocates capital into xSTOCK SPY — tokenized S&P 500 exposure traded on-chain. Swaps are executed through Jupiter aggregator at best available price.

Returns reflect directly in the aCain token price. Share price floats with vault NAV: Share Price = Total Vault Value / Total Cain Supply. Users redeem USDC equivalent at any time after the 7-day lock period.

Risk FAQ

What are the primary risks?
Equity market risk (S&P 500 can decline), smart contract risk in the Anchor vault program, xSTOCK SPY liquidity risk during off-market hours, and oracle staleness risk. The vault does not hedge downside.
How is the performance fee calculated?
30% applies only to realised profit at withdrawal. Your cost basis is recorded at deposit. If your position is flat or in loss, only the $0.20 flat fee is deducted.
What is the 7-day lock period?
Withdrawals are locked for 7 days after each deposit, enforced on-chain by the Anchor program. This is not a frontend restriction — it cannot be bypassed. Each deposit slot has its own independent lock timer.
