About

The aCain vault accepts USDC deposits on Solana and allocates 100% of capital into xSTOCK SPY — a tokenized representation of the S&P 500 index traded on-chain via Solana. Swaps are executed automatically through Jupiter aggregator at best available price.

Returns are reflected directly in the aCain token price rather than distributed as yield — providing passive, compounding exposure to U.S. large-cap equities entirely on-chain. Upon redemption, USDC is returned net of a 30% performance fee on profits only.

The vault is designed for investors who want equity-like returns without brokerage accounts, custody friction, or off-chain settlement. Show less

Risk FAQ

What are the primary risks associated with this vault?
The primary risks include equity market risk (the S&P 500 can decline), smart contract risk in the Kamino vault program, xSTOCK SPY liquidity risk during off-market hours, and oracle manipulation risk on the Pyth price feed. The vault does not hedge market downside.
How is the performance fee calculated?
The 30% fee applies only to realised profit at the time of withdrawal. Your cost basis is recorded at deposit. If your position is flat or in loss, only the $0.20 flat fee is deducted. No management fee is charged on AUM.
What happens if xSTOCK SPY has no liquidity?
Outside NYSE trading hours, on-chain liquidity for xSTOCK SPY may be thin. Deposits during these windows are queued and executed at market open to protect against bad fills. Withdrawals follow the same slippage-protected routing via Jupiter aggregator.
